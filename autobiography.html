Autobiography

Birth and Family

Carlynda Amoah is my name and I am 19 years of age. I was born on the 26th day of July 2006 which was a Wednesday giving me the name 'Ekua'. I am a proud Ghanaian and I hail from the Central Region specifically Agona Nyakrom.
I am from a family of 6; my father by name Dr. Amoah, my mother by name Mrs. Amoah, a brother named ELeazar Amoah, 2 sisters named Phaedra and Zaneta and I. I am the first born female of the children and we all reside in Tema Community 25.

Education

Favorite High School is Delhi Private School International(DPSI)

I started my basic education at Trinity Located at Dansoman where I completed my nursery and moved to Westend International school located at Kasoa to complete Kindergarten and primary 1. I then proceeded to Bediako Memorial Institute to further complete primary and the first 2 years of junior high school.
I furthered my high school education at Delhi Private School International, located at Tema community 25, after the covid-19 pandemic where I completed and passed my IGCSE exams sucessfully in May 2023.
I wrote Nov/Dec WASSCE exams in november 2023 and applied to the University of Professional Studies Accra (UPSA) for the 2024/2025 academic year, where I was admitted to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
I am currently in the first semester of my second year at UPSA to the glory of the Lord.

Religion

A short message about Faith from the Apostle Arome Osayi.

I am a devoted Christian and I worship at the Glorious Tidings Prayer Ministry, located at Teshie. I was born again on the 26th of February, 2025 and have been serving the Lord ever since. I am a member of the youth ministry.
I believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God and that He died on the cross for the remission of my sins and rose again on the third day to give me eternal life. I love the Lord and I intend to serve Him all the days of my life.
I believe in the the word of God and in the power of prayer and fasting and I make sure I fast and pray atleast once a week to connect with God.
I also ensure I read my bible daily to know the word of God and grow in the knowledge of Him and His ways.

Social

I am an introverted person, but I love making new friends and socializing with people. I love helping people in need and I am always ready to lend a listening ear to anyone who needs to talk.
In my free time, I love to read books, watch movies and listen to music.
I love baking and cooking as well. I often try out new recipes and experiment with different ingredients to create delicious meals and desserts.
I prefer to be indoors most of the time unless I am going out with family or for a church activity.
It is my greatest desire to draw people closer to God through my actions and words and encourage them to live a life that is pleasing to Him without giving up on the Lord.
